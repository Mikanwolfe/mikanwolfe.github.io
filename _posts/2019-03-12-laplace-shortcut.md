## ## Laplace Final Value Theorem

This formula is used to find the steady-state value of a system:

$$
\lim_{t\to\infty}f(t)=\lim_{s\to0}sF(s)
$$

The initial value theorem:

$$
f(0+)=\lim_{s\to\infty}sF(s)
$$
